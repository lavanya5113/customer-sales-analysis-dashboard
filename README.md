Customer Sales & Behavior Analysis Dashboard

Project Overview

    1. This project focuses on analyzing customer shopping behavior using Excel and Power BI.
    2. The analysis was performed to understand customer purchasing patterns, revenue contribution, product category performance, and payment preferences. 
    3. Interactive dashboards were created to visualize the insights and support business decision-making.

Objectives

    1. Analyze customer behavior based on gender and age group
    2. Identify high-revenue customer segments
    3. Study product category performance
    4. Analyze customer payment preferences
    5. Generate business insights for improving sales strategies
   
Tools & Technologies Used

    1. Excel
    2. Power BI
    3. Pivot Table
    4. Power Query
    5. DAX
    6. Data Cleaning & Transformation
   
Data Preparation

    1. Cleaned and transformed raw customer data
    2. Created age group categories for analysis
    3. Extracted year information from invoice date
    4. Calculated revenue using DAX measures
    5. Prepared data for dashboard visualization
   
Key Insights

Customer Analysis

     1. Female customers contributed the highest revenue and purchase quantity
     2. The 35–55 age group showed the highest shopping activity
     3. Younger customers preferred digital payment methods
Category Performance

     1. Clothing had the highest purchase volume
     2. Technology products generated higher revenue per transaction
     3. Cosmetics and clothing categories were dominated by female customers
Payment Analysis

     1. Cash was the most preferred payment method
     2. Credit cards were commonly used for high-value purchases
     3. Digital payment adoption was higher among younger customers
   
Business Recommendations

    1. Focus marketing campaigns on female customers
    2. Target the 35–55 age group with personalized offers
    3. Increase promotions for Clothing and Technology categories
    4. Encourage digital payments through discounts and reward programs
    5. Introduce loyalty programs for repeat customers
     
Dashboard Features

    1. Revenue analysis dashboard
    2. Shopping distribution by gender and age group
    3. Product category performance analysis
    4. Payment method analysis
    5. Interactive visual reports using Power BI
